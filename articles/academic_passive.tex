\section{Academic Passive}
\subsection{Используется со словами}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item \bld{Say}
    \item \bld{Believe}
    \item \bld{Think}
    \item \bld{Report}
    \item \bld{Estimate}
    \item \bld{Know}
    \item \bld{Expect}
\end{enumerate}
\subsection{Структура}
\p
Говорят, утверждают, что \dots\\
\sample{It is said that he is rich}\\
\sample{He is said to be rich}\\
\sample{The fire is thought to have started at about 8 o'clock}\\
Вместо \bld{is} могут быть \bld{was/has been}\\
\sample{At least 10000 dolphins have been reported to be caught in the nets of tuna fishers this year}\\
\sample{At least 10000 dolphins are reported to have been caught in the nets of tuna fishers this year}
